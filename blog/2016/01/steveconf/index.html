Keynote - A new approach to learning

For the last year and a half, I've been working hard on the same project. As a lifetime learner with a family and hobbies other than 1s and 0s, it has been hard to find extended periods of time to learn new things. If a sprint that went quicker than usual, maybe I'd work in a few hours of research time. But a few hours is often not enough time to do anything other than watch a video or two; very little hands-on time for exploration.

But that ends now! My year and half long project is done. I am now in between projects, and so it's time to start learning.

For a while now I've been gathering videos and tutorials in an Instapaper folder named "steveconf". My plan is to treat my research time like it is my own private conference. There will be no filler sessions at this conference - only sessions that I am interested in attending.

I'll track all of the sessions here at pleepleus88.github.io.
